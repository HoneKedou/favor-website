Prime Minister impressed by Highsoft

According to Dagens Næringsliv, the Norwegian Prime Minister, Erna Solberg, speaks warmly about Highsoft these days! In a press conference on 18 June she stated that she has met several interesting companies in Norway with the potential of expanding, and she is highly impressed by their achievements. “Wow, do we really do this in Norway?” she says. The Prime Minister emphasizes Highsoft AS and its development: starting with “one man with a good idea” it has developed into a company with 10 employees and a turnover of 30,9 million Norwegian kroners (5.0 million USD) in 2013.
