How can we help you?

I have a general technical question


Highcharts Forum
In the Highcharts Forum you'll find questions and answers, and can discuss anything with the Highcharts developers directly. A large community is ready to help you, and our support engineers monitor the forum and attends to unanswered questions daily on business days.
The Highsoft Forum has 14,281 users who have posted 55,438 posts in 13,804 different topics

I have a specific programming question


Stack Overflow
To get help on a specific question or problem, do a search or post a question to Stack Overflow with the tag "highcharts" or "highstock". We monitor these questions and answer daily. Additionally, an army of developers are ready to help you and generally you'll receive help quicker than in the forum.
8,488 Stack Overflow questions are tagged Highcharts or Highstock

I want to contact you directly


Email
To contact us through our helpdesk, just send an email to
[email protected]. One of our support engineers will answer you within approximately 24 hours on business days.

I want to request a feature


Uservoice
To request a feature, please post a suggestion on UserVoice, or vote for the ones that alre already registered.
689 new features are currently requested for Highcharts

I have found a bug, and I have reproduction steps


GitHub Issues
Read our rules for issue reporting and report it on GitHub Issues. If you're not sure it's a bug, please check with us in one of the above channels first.
The highcharts.com repo is starred by 2,155 and forked by 931 users

Maintenance and Support

These channels are available for our customers with a Maintenance and Support Agreement:

  • To contact us directly through our ticket system and ensure your guaranteed 36 hours response time, send an email to [email protected] and make sure you enter your LicenseID number in the subject.
  • To chat with our support team, contact us by Skype on HighchartsSupport. When prompted for a message to send along with the contact request, it is important that you insert your LicenseID number. We are available on chat between 10:00 and 18:00 CET, (Central European Time) business days.

Custom implementation services

Through our partners, Black Label, we offer help on customizing Highstock or Highcharts so that they look and behave exactly like you want them to. Black Label have been doing that on a daily basis as a partner of Highsoft since 2010. Black Label offer any development service, from quick chart design brush-up to working closely with the rest of your development team on implementing across systems.
