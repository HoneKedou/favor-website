Ecodrifter is a simple and clean logo sample with a visual language and feel towards ecological organizations or companies which have an eco-friendly business relation.

This product bundle contains:

  • 1 .AI - Illustrator file with the same sample dispositions as above

  • 1 .PDF - Adobe Acrobat file with the same sample dispositions as above
