Description:

Monchamp is a logo template that matches Real Estate & Property management, as well as architecture needs. It's intention is to represent property area, real estate boundaries, terrain, construction, mortgage consultancy, realtor and arquitecture in general.

RECOMMENDATION:

Recommended is to use this logo for the following sectors:

  • Real Estate
  • Property Management
  • Realtors
  • Mortgage Consultancy
  • Construction
  • Architecture

File specifications:

The logo is made in 2 version, with 2 background color variations:

  • Vertical approach over white and dark background
  • Hoizontal approach over white and dark background.

FILE TYPES:

All files are vector data for .PSD, .EPS, .AI and .PNG (Adobe-Fireworks with pages). CS2+ compatible

FONTS:

The fonts used are "Droid Serif" in Bold and Regular style

COLORS:

Colors used are:

  • Bright Green: #b7d60b;
  • Medium Green: #709d04;
  • Linear Gradient: #709d04 to #2b5603;
  • Dark Green: #1a4403;
