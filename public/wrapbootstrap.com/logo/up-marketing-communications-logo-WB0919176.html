I've been designing logos for about 28 years now and practically speaking you need something that:

  • Reduces Well
  • Can be used on a variety of media: vinyl, embroidery, mugs, pens, offset, and online

High end logos -- logos of the biggest companies with NY ad budgets -- have logos that are flat for the most part. There's no shine, shadows, or gradients. These don't reproduce well across media types.

This logo can be used for marketing communication, travel, or IT. It can be used for a freelance writer too. It represents forward, travel, highway, ideas, and awakening. General, good feeling stuff.

It comes in RGB and CMYK and the Pantone colors are:

  • Pantone Orange 021 C
  • Pantone 7544 C

FONT: Raleways Regular

The CMYK orange is NOT a translation of the Pantone 021 C, it's a build of 80% magenta and 100% yellow, this works out better but you might want to tweak it for your needs. Get a Color Bridge book.
