Description:

Voyant is a logo template that matches pretty much any company purpose. It's intention is to represent dynamic flow, connectivity, communication and networking

RECOMMENDATION:

The recommendations is to apply it for the following sectors:

  • Communications
  • Technology
  • General Industry with services connected to dynamic flows such as engines, transportation.
  • Chemistry
  • Community/technology support
  • Network/Cable, connectivity

File specifications:

The logo is made in 2 version, with 2 background color variations:

  • Vertical approach over white and dark background
  • Hoizontal approach over white and dark background.

FILE TYPES:

All files are vector data for .PSD, .EPS and .AI (version CS2+)

FONTS:

The font used is "Raleway" in Heavy and Bold style.

COLORS:

Colors used are:

  • White: #FFFFFF;
  • Dark Blue: #001943;
  • Bright Blue: #006cb7;
