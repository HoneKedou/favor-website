Description:

Yumi-Shin is a logo template that matches oriental/Asiatic style. It's intention is to represent oriental culture, knowledge, art and tradition

RECOMMENDATION:

Recommended is to use this logo for the following sectors:

  • Restaurant (Japanese - Chinese Cuisine)
  • Yoga
  • Oriental/Asiatic Arts
  • Tea-House
  • Martial-Arts
  • Community entrepreneurship towards Asiatic culture

File specifications:

The logo is made in 2 version, with 2 background color variations:

  • Vertical approach over white and dark background
  • Hoizontal approach over white and dark background.

FILE TYPES:

All files are vector data for .PSD, .EPS, .AI and .PNG (Adobe-Fireworks with pages)

FONTS:

The fonts used are "DejaVu Serif" + "Open-Sans"

COLORS:

Colors used are:

  • Dark Red: #360000;
