Jun 2, 2010 • General Stuffs → freebies

Business Card Specs

  • Sizes: 2 x 3.5”, 2 x 2” (square card) or 1.5 x 3.5” (slim card)

  • Specifications: Full Color Both Sides
  • Shipping: FREE UPS Ground Shipping

How To Join?

  • You must be 18 years or older.
  • Limited to US residents only.
  • Write a comment in this post.
  • One entry per person. Duplicate comments will be voided.

Comment entries will be accepted until June 18, 2010. The 10 winners will be selected and contacted by email. Each winner will receive 1,000 business card printing from Uprinting.
