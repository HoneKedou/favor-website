Japan Earthquake Poster 102

Apr 18, 2011 • General Stuffs → updates

Recently I've joined a group of artists to support Japan earthquake relief efforts. Each artist contributed a piece of art work dedicated to raise funds. The artworks will be displayed and available for purchase at the To Japan with Love (Facebook event) art show which is organized by Linda Nakanishi. All proceeds from the show will be donated to Canadian Red Cross.

My Poster Design

The Japanese symbol in the illustration means home. It symbolize the thousands of homes and families that were destroyed and washed away by the earthquake and tsunami which happened in Japan on March 11, 2011.

Buy & Support → Society6

To support Japan earthquake relief efforts, please buy the art at Society6. It is available in various poster sizes, iPhone case, iPad case, and tshirt. All proceeds will be donated to charity.

Art Show: To Japan With Love

May 4 2011 @ 7pm to 10pm — Function13 Gallery

The gallery will showcase a 24 x 36" of all posters. A limited of prints (12 x 18") and postcards (4 x 6") will be available for sale. All net proceeds from the show will be donated to the Canadian Red Cross for relief efforts in Japan. Visit tojapanwithlove.ca and this Facebook event for more details. If you are by Toronto area, please come and join this event. Everyone is invited.
