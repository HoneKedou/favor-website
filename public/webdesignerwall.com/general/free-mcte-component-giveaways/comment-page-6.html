Free MCTE Component Giveaways 608

Apr 23, 2008 • General Stuffs → freebies

Previously we did a free TxEff giveaway contest and the feedbacks were very well received. Jumpeye Components would like to hear more feedbacks from you guys. This time they are giving out the MCTE component. To enter the contest, just write your feedback in the comment form for this post and everyone will get a free copy of MCTE V3 (value $50). In addition, we will draw three random numbers from the comment list to giveaway the MCTE Collection Plus (value $200).

How does this work?

  • Everyone who entered a comment will get a copy of MCTE V3 (value $50).
  • Three winners will win a copy of MCTE Collection Plus (value $200). We will generate three random numbers from the comment list and the three numbers generated will be the winners.
  • Comment entries will be accepted until May 4, 2008 (at 12am).

How to enter?

  • Add a comment to this post. Tell what do you think about MCTE.
  • One comment per person. Duplicated comments will be void and deleted.

What is MCTE?

With MCTE, you can instantly create any kind of MovieClip transition effects that can be used in slide shows and photo galleries. Here are some sample effects:

  • Cloud
  • Desertillusion
  • Flip Page
  • Swap
  • Ripple Alpha
  • Square Light
  • Switch
  • Waterdrops
  • Genie
  • Pixelate
  • 3D Cube
  • Blur
