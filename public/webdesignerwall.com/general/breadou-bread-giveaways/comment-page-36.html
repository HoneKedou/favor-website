Breadou Bread Giveaways 722

Jan 19, 2009 • General Stuffs → freebies

Folks! Are you feeling a little hungry? We have something to give you that can perhaps fullfil your hunger (or maybe not). We are giving out 5 wrist-rest products by Breadou, that look and smell like real bread. They are made from memory foam that can be used as a wrist rest or stress ball. Or use them to trick your co-workers and friends by offering Breadou as a snack. Submit a comment in this post before Feb 3rd, 2009 to win a free Breadou of your choice.

Update: The contest is closed and the winners are: 33, 262, 372, 571, and 620.

Pick Your Bread

Pick one bread (either Emoticon or Loaf) from the product list (visit the Breadou's product page for more details) below and briefly explain why you want it. If you are the lucky winner, that Breadou is yours to keep (or eat).

Emoticon


Happy Coffee Cream, Honey Bit, Nottie Nutti, Vanilla Dream, Hot n Spicy, and Berry Sweetie.

Loaf


French Lovin, Sugar Braid, Cheesy Pumpkin, and Custard Caterpillar.

Contest Rules

  • Remember: pick one product and enter it along with your comment entry. Tell us why you want it.
  • One entry per person. Duplicate comments will be voided and deleted.
  • Winners will be contacted by email (so make sure your email address is correct).
  • Comment entries will be accepted until Feb 3rd, 2009. Then we will randomly generate 5 winning numbers from the comment list. The winners will receive a Breadou.
  • Breaoud will be shipped via USPS Priority Mail for domestic in the US, and via USPS First-Class Mail International Package for International Delivery.
