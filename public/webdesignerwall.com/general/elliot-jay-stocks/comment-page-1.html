Elliot Jay Stocks 56

Aug 23, 2007 • General Stuffs → talented people

Elliot Jay Stocks is Senior Designer at Carson Systems and well known on the web for his visually rich websites and intricate attention to detail. His work is regularly featured in online and offline publications, showcased on numerous design 'inspiration' websites, and used as an example of how accessible web design can still look beautiful.

Location: Twickenham, UK
URL: elliotjaystocks.com

Elliot writes regularly for several internationally published magazines and has recently turned his hand - or rather his voice - to public speaking. In his somewhat limited spare time he pretends to be a musician and would also love to start drawing again.
