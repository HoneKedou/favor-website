Microsoft Event & Free Giveaways 332

May 29, 2009 • General Stuffs → event, freebies

Hi guys! I have these amazing giveaways for you, sponsored by Microsoft. Before I go to the prizes, I would like to mention that Microsoft is having a web event called Make Web, Not War '09 in Toronto on Jun 10th (full day), and Vancouver on Jun 2nd (half day). Visit their site for details and registration. Write a comment about the event before Jun 12, in this post, for a chance to win: Microsoft Expression Studio, Gears of War 2 (Xbox game), or 250GB LaCie External Drive.

Prizes:


  • Expression Studio

  • Gears of War 2

  • 250GB LaCie Drive

How to win a prize?

  • Post your comment about the event in this post.
  • One entry per person. Duplicate comments will be voided and deleted.
  • Winners will be contacted by email.

Comment entries will be accepted until June 12, 2009. Then three winning numbers will be generated randomly from the comment list. First number will get the first prize, second number will get the second prize, and third number will get the third prize.
