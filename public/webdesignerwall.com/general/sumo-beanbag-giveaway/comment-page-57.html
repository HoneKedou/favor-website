Jul 20, 2009 • General Stuffs → freebies

Are you getting tired from working hard on your computer? Well, take a break and enter a comment in this post for your chance to win a free beanbag chair sponsored by Sumo. It is a super-sized beanbag that is perfect for playing games and relaxing in your lounge. I've been using Sumo beanbags for several years, so I can tell you they are quality products. Enter a comment now before August 7, 2009.

Update: This contest is closed and the winner is: #176.

How to win a bean bag?

  • Pick one product (Gamer, Couple, or Sultan) & color, and briefy explain why you want it.
  • One entry per person. Duplicate comments will be voided.
  • Winners will be contacted by email.

Comment entries will be accepted until August 7, 2009. Then one winning number will be generated randomly from the comment list. The bean bag will be delivered by Sumo.
