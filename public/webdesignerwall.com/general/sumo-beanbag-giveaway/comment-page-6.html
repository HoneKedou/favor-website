Are you getting tired from working hard on your computer? Well, take a break and enter a comment in this post for your chance to win a free beanbag chair sponsored by Sumo. Enter a comment now before August 7, 2009.

How to win a bean bag?

  • Pick one product (Gamer, Couple, or Sultan) & color, and briefy explain why you want it.
  • One entry per person. Duplicate comments will be voided.
  • Winners will be contacted by email.

Comment entries will be accepted until August 7, 2009. Then one winning number will be generated randomly from the comment list. The bean bag will be delivered by Sumo.
