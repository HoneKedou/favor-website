Free TxEff Component Giveaways 1,321

Jan 7, 2008 • General Stuffs → freebies

Web Designer Wall is giving out freebies again! This time we are giving out 3 license copies of TxEff, sponsored by Jump Eye Components. TxEff is a Flash Component that allows you to create stunning text animations without ActionScript skills (read review here). To enter the contest, add a comment in this post and you could win one of the three TxEff component giveaways (value $169.97). All comments must be submitted before January 25, 2008.

How does this work?

Comment entries will be accepted until January 25, 2008 before 12am. We will generate three random numbers from the comment list and the three numbers generated will be the winners. The winners will be announced on January 28, 2008.

How to enter?

  • Add a comment to this post. Tell us what do you think about TxEff.
  • One comment per person. Duplicated comments will be void and deleted.
