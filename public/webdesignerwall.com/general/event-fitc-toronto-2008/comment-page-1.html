Event: FITC Toronto 2008 39

Mar 25, 2008 • General Stuffs → event

If you are serious about your web career, don't miss out on this upcoming festival in Toronto - FITC 2008. It will take place between April 19th - 22th. For those who don't know about the festival, "FITC is now entering its 7th amazing year, and is the only festival of its kind; combining cutting edge technical and creative speakers from around the world". The speaker list is endless, so I'm just going to list my favorite ones: Si Scott, Joshua Davis, Rob Chiu, Scott Hansen, and Julian Dolce (see full Speaker List).

Discount Coupon

This year Web Designer Wall is one of their media supporters and we are giving out 10% discount coupon code: WDW03122 for anyone who is interested in purchasing the tickets (see festival and workshop pricing).

Who Should Attend?

If you are interested in the latest Flash technology or you are an entrepreneur looking for new business ideas in the web industry. Presentations are organized in four categories: Panel, Creative, Technical, and Business. So it pretty much covers everything from technical to business perspective.

I went to FITC two years ago and my experience was amazing. It changed my perspective toward the web industry in both design and business aspect. Listening to the advices from the professional speakers can open up window to new ideas and approaches. So I totally recommend this festival.

More Information

For more details, visit FITC.ca (don't forget to enter the coupon code: WDW03122).
