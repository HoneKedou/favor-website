Giveaways: 2012 FITC Tickets 13

Feb 22, 2012 • General Stuffs → event, freebies

I've got two FITC Toronto 2012 festival tickets to giveaway and all you need to do is send one tweet for your chance to win. For those of you who don't know what FITC Toronto is, it's a design and technology focussed conference which is comprised of three days of learning, networking and partying, April 23-25. I will be attending the event as usual. This year's event has features over 70 renowned digital creators from around the globe, covering everything from HTML5 to making digital art.

How to enter

To enter for your chance to win a festival ticket, all you need to do is tweet the name of one of the speakers by March 24, 2012 with hashtag #designtechcon to enter. Winners will be announced on Twitter, so be sure to follow @fitc and @nickla. If you are interested in purchasing tickets to the event, I have a special coupon for you: enter "webdesignerwall" to save $100 off festival, works and flex passes (not valid for student tickets).

Update: the contest is closed and the winners are: @joim and @alicetheblue
