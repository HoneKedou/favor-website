Jan 28, 2010 • General Stuffs → freebies

Exciting contest: InMotion Hosting is giving away 3 Power accounts (one-year) to WDW's readers. To enter this contest, simply post a comment in this post before Feb 12, 2010. I've moved my hosting to them for about 2 weeks and very pleased with their services, particularly their 24/7 tech support. If you are looking for web hosting, check them out. They have a wide range of hosting packages from $5.95/mth shared hosting to $199/mth dedicated servers.

How to win a 1-year hosting account?

  • Visit InMotion Hosting and write down which hosting package(s) best interest to you.
  • One entry per person. Duplicate comments will be voided.
  • Winners will be contacted by email.

Comment entries will be accepted until Feb 12, 2010. Then three winning numbers will be generated randomly from the comment list.
