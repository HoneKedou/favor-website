Giveaways: Printing Credits 256

Mar 12, 2010 • General Stuffs → freebies

Yes, more giveaways for you! Next Day Flyers is giving away printing credits for you to print anything of your choice. Next Day Flyers offers wide range of printing services from business card printing to brochure printing. First prize is $50, second & third prize is $25. You must be 18 years or older in order to participate. Contest will end on March 26th, 2010.

Update: The winners are: 174, 194, and 223.

How To Join?

  • You must be 18 years or older.
  • Write a comment in this post.
  • One entry per person. Duplicate comments will be voided.

Comment entries will be accepted until March 26, 2010. The winners will be selected and contacted by email.
