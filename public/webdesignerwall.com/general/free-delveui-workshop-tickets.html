Free DelveUI Workshop Tickets 76

Jul 26, 2009 • General Stuffs → event, freebies

I have 5 last minute DelveUI workshop tickets to give out. Comment in this post before Jul 31, 2009 for your chance to win one. DelveUI is a two-day workshop that focuses on UI design. The event will take place August 5 and 6, 2009 at the Pfizer Auditorium in Brooklyn. This is their first year, but the speaker list is impressive. Visit delvenyc.com for more details.

How to win a ticket?

  • Post your comment and briefly explain why you want to attend.
  • One entry per person. Duplicate comments will be voided.
  • Winners will be contacted by email.

Comment entries will be accepted until July 31, 2009. Then 5 winning numbers will be generated randomly from the comment list. Each winner will get a DelveUI workshop ticket.
