PhotoFlow Component Giveaways 768

Aug 16, 2007 • General Stuffs → freebies

First of all, we would like to thank Flashloaded, who provided us with the photoFlow component to complete the PhotoFlip Gallery tutorial. In addition, we are giving out 3 licensed copies of photoFlow. Just add a comment in this post and you could win one of the three photoFlow component giveaways (value $59.95). All comments must be submitted before August 31, 2007 at 12am.

How does this work?

Comment entries will be accepted until August 31, 2007 at 12am (GMT -5 hrs). We will randomly generate three winning numbers from the comment list. Each winner will win a copy of photoFlow component. The winners will be announced on September 1, 2007.

How to enter?

  • Add a comment to this post, simply say something or "I want photoFlow"
  • One comment per person (submit once only and do not reply any comment here). Duplicated comments will be void.

Don't forget to invite your friends to enter!

Update: The contest is closed and the winners are:

  • 75 - Leandro Costa
  • 334 - Nii Nortey
  • 380 - skyjuice

Congrats to the winners and thank you for your participation. Winners will be contacted by email.
